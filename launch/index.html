The Orpheus is running on damaged systems and borrowed time. Balance its remaining resources, rebuild the main systems, survive emergency events, and recover a route out of the Cosmic Abyss.

Cosmic Abyss is heavily inspired by Ravaged Space, the original text-based incremental survival game created by Steven Lambert.

01

Stabilize resources

Generate Power, cycle Life Support, and mine Processing material. Every action produces one resource while consuming others.

02

Repair the ship

Restore Engineering, Guidance, Engines, and Sensors. Repairs add permanent system load, deducted from Power or Life Support every 20 seconds.

03

Escape the abyss

Bring every main system to ONLINE status while keeping every critical resource above zero. Special operations unlock as repairs progress.

Resource logic

  • Power / Life Support / Processing fuel repair actions and must never reach zero.
  • Radioactive is an inverse resource. It begins at zero and rises through failed leaks or dangerous offers.
  • Amount / Auto / Speed modifications increase output, automate actions, or reduce cooldowns.

Emergency channel

  • Respond to robot exchanges with Y or N.
  • Enter displayed four-digit codes before the ten-second window closes.
  • Failed hazards damage resources; failed radioactive leaks increase contamination.

Operator controls

  • P L O E G N S activate systems directly.
  • A Amount, U Automation, and D Speed install modifications.
